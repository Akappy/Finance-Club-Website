Previous Speakers

Peter Thompson is the Founder of Magis Leadership Advisors, LLC, a Chicago-based firm focused on advising leaders, owners and senior management of financial services companies in business strategy and the recruitment and development of key executives. Prior to founding Magis, from 2009-2015, Peter served as Chief Executive Officer of Perkins Investment Management, an investment management firm that managed in excess of $22 billion in assets under management. Additionally, he served as Chairman of the company's Board of Directors. He has also served as Chicago Mayor Richard M. Daley's Campaign Finance Director for the Mayor's successful and final re-election in 2007.

Susie Hultquist is a former Portfolio Manager and Security Analyst at Columbia Wanger Asset Management where she focused on small and mid-cap consumer discretionary stocks with strong growth potential and helped manage $40 billion in assets. She previously served as a Senior Analyst at Bank of America in the Global Credit and Special Situations Group. Since leaving Columbia Wanger Asset Management, Susie has been working to create her own tech startup.
