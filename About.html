What

The Finance Club was founded in 2014 by Alec Kaplan and Schuler Small in order to teach students about the stock market and investing, provide opportunities to trade stocks in a tangible portfolio, and connect club members with real world experts.

The Finance Club consists of members from all grade levels. There are no requirements or prerequisites to join. All U-High students with an interest in finance and investing are welcome to join.

Please contact alecrkaplan@gmail.com for more information or questions.
